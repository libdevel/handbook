Guide Requirements

Librarians work closely with instructors to decide the best resources and organization for a course guide. However, Librarians must design, build, and maintain that course guide, as instructors are not eligible for LibGuides accounts. See our FAQ Page for more information.

The Right Tool...
Course guides should focus on course-related resources. Things like assignments, grading rubrics, and most course-specific documents are best embedded in learning management software such as Sakai.

Requirements At-a-glance

1 Guide navigation must be based on the Course Guide template.
2 Guide must be associated with an instructor(s) and instructor's last name must be listed parenthetically in the title. Librarian's last name may not be in the title.
3 Structure of pages and boxes decided entirely by librarian and instructor.
4 Only the most recent version of any instructor's guide may be published. Versions from previous semesters/years must be unpublished.
5 Must complement rather than compete with any LMS or portfolio software.

Title Formatter

Course titles must conform to the approved format. Librarians should not add their names to the course titles.
