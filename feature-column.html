Columns

Use columns where appropriate to structure the content in your Rich Text / HTML boxes.

  • Simple Column Generator
  • 3-column (List)
  • 3-column (Images)
  •  Back to top

Simple Column Generator

In certain cases, you may wish to divide your box into columns. Examples might include: A list spread over multiple columns, columns of images and/or text, or the Course Overview Box for course guides.

Limitations of Columns
System Content items such as books, databases, documents, links, or media widgets take up the width of an entire box and may not be divided into columns.
