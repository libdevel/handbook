Guide Creation Questions

Consider these broad questions as you plan your guide.

 

What is the purpose of the Guide? Does it address both the mission of the Library to provide critical information literacy with the particular needs of users?

Who are the primary, secondary, and tertiary audiences for this guide?

To what uses will the guide be put? Will it be used in instruction? By self-guided users?

How does the guide relate to- and integrate with other guides?

How often will the guide need to be updated and who will update it?
