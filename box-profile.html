Profile Box

Subject, Course, and How-to guides are focused around a Librarian, whose profile box is located underneath the left navigation menu on each page of the guide.

Ask Us About Profile Boxes
Profile boxes are standardized by the LibGuides Management Team. Please contact the LMT if you require edits to your profile box.

Profile Box Explained

All profile boxes must feature:

1 Title
2 Photo (min width 250px)   Optional
3 Name
4 Email Me button
5 Link to Subject/Location Pathfinder Page
6 Address
7 Phone and Email
8 Schedule a Meeting Button   Optional
9 Subject specialties   Automatically Assigned
10 Social and Professional Media Icons   Optional

Profile Page

Profile pages are automatically generated.

Profile Page Explained

All profile pages must feature a Profile Box, a List of Published Guides, and a Brief Bio of the Librian. We recommend including at least relevant degrees and subject specializations.
