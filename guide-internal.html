Guide Requirements

Internal Guides are for use by Library personnel only and are not to be shared with the public. As such, they are not bound by any template or style requirements, although we recommend the Internal Guide template. Setting internal guides to “private” will delist them from the list of published guides and hide them from users who do not already have the URL.

Good Use Case
A good use case for an internal guide might be a training guide for employees of a particular department or team.

Requirements At-a-glance

1 Must remain 'Private' (unpublished)
2 Must have password protection for sensitive information
3 May use any template
4 May use any colors and fonts
