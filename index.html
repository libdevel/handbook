Create the Guide

Copy from the General Subject Template
1 Click  Create Guide
2 Choose Copy content/layout from an existing guide from the Choose Layout or Reuse dropdown.
3 Choose General Subject Template from the Select a Guide dropdown.
4 Type the guide name in the Guide Name field (e.g., 'Cultural Studies').

Contact the LMT
The names and URLs of General Subject guides are managed by the LibGuides Management Team. Please contact the LMT before proceeding with this step.
5 Choose Subject from the Guide Type dropdown.
6 Ignore all other default settings and click Create Guide.

Noting the guide requirements for information architecture...
